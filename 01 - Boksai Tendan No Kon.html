1. Open
    1. Stand toward N
    2. Slight bend in knees, raise bo with right hand
    3. Left knife-hand strike, toward W, turn palm up, dynamic reach for bo near right armpit
  2. Ready
    1. Unwind hands as you turn toward E in ready stance
  3. Punch
    1. Look toward N, raise bo to shoulder height
    2. As you move into a right front stance toward N, punch right
  4. First kiai
    1. Still in front stance: up, down, right, left
    2. Bring bo backward and lift front foot
    3. Thrust and bring down front foot, kiai
  5. First uppercut
    1. Still in right front stance, reverse strike, strike
    2. As you bring the back foot up halfway, row backwards on the right
    3. Move the right foot to a left hook stance, finishing the bo with an uppercut
  6. Second kiai
    1. Unwind by pivoting on the feet to turn facing S while performing a reverse strike
    2. Bring bo on right shoulder and punch while moving to right front stance, kiai
